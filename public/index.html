Affiliate marketing

Affiliate marketing is typically done through social media engagement that promotes a particular product or service and relies heavily on social media platforms like Instagram, YouTube, and Snapchat to reach a wider audience. We research, identify and evaluate influencers based on brief and optimise and promote campaigns on multiple platforms for better engagement like instagram etc.

Collaborations

Finding relevant collaboration for your brand is a part of a successful influencer marketing strategy. Collaborative posts typically involve influencers creating sponsored content featuring your brand's products or services seamlessly in various forms, including product reviews or sponsored giveaways. Clients hire and collaborate with influencers and optimise campaigns on multiple platforms.

Influencer management

Influencer management is handling influencer-based marketing strategies which are created with the aim of guaranteeing that any partnered influencers consistently put out high-quality content that furthers your company’s goals along with balancing the needs of the influencers. We ensure regularly communicating with influencers to ensure everything is progressing as it should.
